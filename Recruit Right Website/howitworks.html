How Recruit Right Works

Our AI-powered platform streamlines every step of the recruitment process

1

Job Creation

Organizations create job listings with detailed requirements, skills, and qualifications. Our AI analyzes these to create optimized matching criteria.

2

Resume Screening

Upload candidate resumes to our platform, where our AI analyzes and ranks them based on job requirements, skills, and experience match.

3

Candidate Registration

Candidates create profiles, upload resumes, and specify preferences. They can also practice with our AI-powered interview simulations.

4

Interview Scheduling

Our platform automatically suggests interview times based on interviewer and candidate availability, streamlining the scheduling process.

5

Structured Interviews

Interviewers use our guided framework to conduct fair and consistent interviews. The AI helps with question suggestions and evaluation criteria.

6

Feedback & Evaluation

After interviews, our platform collects structured feedback from interviewers and provides objective candidate evaluations.
